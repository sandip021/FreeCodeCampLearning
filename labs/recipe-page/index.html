Cheese Toastie

The cheese toastie is the ultimate comfort classic: a golden, butter-crisped sandwich that hides a center of molten, gooey cheese. Simple to make yet incredibly satisfying, it's a timeless favorite that turns a few basic pantry staples into a warm, soul-soothing meal.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of thick white bread
  • Salted butter, softened
  • Grated or sliced cheddar cheese
  • A pinch of sea salt (optional, for the crust)

Instructions

  1. Spread softened butter generously onto one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place one slice butter-side down in a pan and top with plenty of cheese.
  3. Cover with the second slice (butter-side up) and fry over medium-low heat until golden.
  4. Flip the toastie and cook until both sides are crisp and the cheese is fully melted.
